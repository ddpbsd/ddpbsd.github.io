Tasks¶

Get a list of tasks¶

GET /_tasks

Get a list of tasks that are searches¶

GET /_tasks?actions=*search&detailed

Get information on a specific task¶

Get the TASK_ID from one of the above queries.

GET /_tasks/TASK_ID
