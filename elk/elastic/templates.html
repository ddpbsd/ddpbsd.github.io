Templates¶

List templates¶

curl -s http://elastic0:9200/_cat/templates

View a template¶

curl -s http://elastic0:9200/_template/template_name

Upload a template¶

curl -s -XPUT -H 'Content-Type: application/json' http://elastic0:9200/_template/bro-dns -d @template_file
