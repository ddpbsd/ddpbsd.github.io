Mission BBQ¶

Warning

The sauces are always in the wrong locations. Pay attention to the labels on the bottles.

Sauces¶

  • Tupelo honey heat is great.

  • Texas twang is peppery goodness.

  • Smoky Mountain is a classic BBQ sauce.

  • Don’t forget the Baja Bold sauce.

Meat¶

  • Get the pulled pork.

  • NC style sandwiches have cold slaw on them (the best way to use the cold slaw).

Sides¶

  • Cold Slaw is meh.

  • Cheesy potatoes are awesome.

  • Cornbread is necessary (although it may be a little dry).

Desserts¶

  • Always get a brownie.
