Planting¶

We bought a large planter (with legs!) for the herbs. It holds the water underneath everything with a little water guage to know when to water the plants. Mint will be by itself because it’s a weed and will overtake everything.

We bought more beefsteak tomato plants than we thought, so I need more large planters and tomato cages.
