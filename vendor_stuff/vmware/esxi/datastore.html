Don’t fill the disk¶

ESXi needs space for swap, so don’t fill it too much. Not sure how much is the sweet spot though.

Snapshots are horrible¶

Snapshots take space. Each iteration is a diff of the previous snapshot or base image I don’t know. So each one will take more and more space. Snapshot, do stuff, verify it works, delete old snapshot.
