Look at certs!¶

Check a private key:¶

openssl rsa -in privateKey.key -check

Check a certificate:¶

openssl x509 -in certificate.crt -text -noout

Check the subject in a certificate:¶

openssl x509 -noout -subject -in certificate.crt
