Check eval time left
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Free evals give 180 days to work with the system.
Check on time left before needing to license or rearm.
Also provides the number of rearms that are left.

.. code-block:: console

   slmgr /dlv

Rearm the eval time
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Reset the 180 day counter.

.. code-block:: console

   slmgr /rearm
