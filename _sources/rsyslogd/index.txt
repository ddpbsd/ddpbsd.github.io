rsyslogd
========


hostname should log to /var/log/hostname instead of /var/log
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. code-block:: console

   -host
   *.info                  /var/log/messages
   +host
   *.info                  /var/log/host/messages

Listen on a unix socket
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. code-block:: console

   $AddUnixListenSocket /var/log/socket
