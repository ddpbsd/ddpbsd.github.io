===========
Random Tips
===========

Disable lightdm and run headless:
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Add the following to `/boot/uEnv.txt`:

.. code-block:: console

   # Disable lightdm
   optargs=text

.. note::

   Tip was from http://pix.cs.olemiss.edu/csci581/beagleBoneTips.txt
