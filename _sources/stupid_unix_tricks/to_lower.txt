.. _stupid_unix_tricks_to_lower::

Convert a string to lower case:
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

tr:

.. code-block:: console

   $ echo "$a" | tr '[:upper:]' '[:lower:]'
   hi all

awk:

.. code-block:: console

   $ echo "$a" | awk '{print tolower($0)}'

perl:

.. code-block:: console

   $ echo "$a" | perl -ne 'print lc'
