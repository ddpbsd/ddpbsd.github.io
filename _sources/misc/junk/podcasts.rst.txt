========
podcasts
========

Podcasts I listen to, in no particular order. Not all of these are family friendly.

Host Stuff Works network
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

`Stuff They Don't Want You to Know <https://www.stufftheydontwantyoutoknow.com/>`_ - Conspiracy realism.

`Ridiculous History <https://www.ridiculoushistoryshow.com/>`_ - Strange historical stuff.

`Part-Time Genius <https://www.parttimegenius.show/>`_ - Lots of random facts.

`The Control Group <https://www.controlgroup.show/>`_ - A 10 part fictional story.

`End of the World with Josh Clark <https://www.theendwithjosh.com/>`_ - There are a lot of possible ends to humanity's story. Here are a few existential threats we face.

`Aaron Mahnke's Cabinet of Curiosities <https://www.curiositiespodcast.com/>`_ - Strange little tales from Aaron Mahnke.


Parcast network
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

`Mythology <https://www.parcast.com/mythology>`_ - Mythological figures. Information and stories.

`Cults <https://www.parcast.com/cults>`_ - Information on the creation and status of various cults throughout history.

`Gone <https://www.parcast.com/gone>`_ - Stuff goes missing, these folks try to figure out where it went.

`Conspiracy Theories <https://www.parcast.com/conspiracy>`_ - What it says on the tin.
